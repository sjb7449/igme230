Individual Page Details

Home/Index

For the home page, I used free mockup images of phones and laptops from the site Mockup World. The image used for the "Let's keep in touch!" banner, which can be found at the bottom of every page, was taken from Unsplash. My index.html is also my gallery page as discussed in previous office hours.

About

As discussed in office hours, I opted to have my image and a brief description about myself to be listed on a different page from my index.html. This page includes both an image of my face and a little blurb about me.

Contact

So, obviously this contact form doesn't store the user's data upon pressing "submit" because I have no database to store said information. Instead, the code here is just meant to act as a sort of prototype, giving an idea of how my ideal contact form would look and (sorta) behave. I had to look at form examples from W3Schools to get a better understanding of how they function.

Resume

For my resume, I refered to this article to learn how to properly code a print media query. I also list my email here as a form of contact information.

Project Information Pages

All mockup images are taken from Mockup World with my work pasted on their screens.

Other Aspects

Navigation

In my navigation, the Behance, LinkIn, and Vimeo are all accounts I plan to make over the winter break; therefore, they do not link to anything currently. However, in the future, they would.

Dropdown Menu

I couldn't find anywhere on the site where I felt a dropdown menu would work with my design, so I included one here so that I could still get credit for having one on my site. I used two W3Schools tutorials to assist me in this: here and here. The hoverable dropdown menu is for viewers on a desktop, and the clickable dropdown is for those visiting the site on their mobile devices.
