About me

My name is Sam. This is my fifth year attending RIT- yes, I'm never leaving. Before my sophomore year of college, I studied Informational Technology within CCIS. While I enjoyed the web-based portions of the curriculum, the majority of the program failed to excite me. It was then when I discovered New Media Design. I would describe the major as a marriage between design and technology- the perfect blend of two of my greatest passions.
